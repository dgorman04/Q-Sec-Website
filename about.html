About Us

Q-Sec is a start-up cybersecurity company, aiming to bring quantum-resistant security solutions to our consumers. We identified that despite there being many cybersecurity companies, none of these companies use quantum computing as part of their security. Quantum computing is believed to be the future of cybersecurity, not only are we progressing with the technological advances being made but we are being proactive against possible cyber attacks on our clients.

Our Aim

Q-Sec aims to redefine the meaning of security, by introducing quantum-powered encryption therefore we are able to protect our clients from any type of cyber attacks. We aim to focus on smaller businesses in the beginning, and expand to larger corporations with a well crafted and tested security defence.
